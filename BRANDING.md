# Mawjood – Branding & Design Style Guide

## 1. Brand Identity
Brand Purpose: Make discovering real businesses in Iraq fast, simple, and trustworthy.  
Brand Values: Trust, Simplicity, Speed, Familiarity, Local relevance.  
Brand Personality: Friendly, Helpful, Direct, Reliable, Modern.  
Tagline: Mawjood. كل شيء قريب منك

## 2. Color Palette
Primary: #00897B  
Primary Light: #4DB6AC  
Neutral: #F5F3E7  
Dark Text: #333333  
Accent Gold: #FFB300  
Error: #D32F2F  

## 3. Logo Direction
- Map pin shaped like the Arabic letter م  
- Simple geometric monogram (M/م)  
- One-color, flat, minimal  

## 4. Typography
Arabic: Cairo or Noto Kufi Arabic  
English: Roboto  

## 5. Iconography
Material Icons, rounded, single-color, clean.  

## 6. Component Style
Rounded 16px corners  
Soft shadows  
Teal primary buttons  
Right-aligned text for RTL  

## 7. Motion Guidelines
Fast transitions (150–250ms)  
Fade and RTL slide animations  
No heavy bounce effects  

## 8. Layout
8px spacing grid  
Clean whitespace  
Max-width 600px on large screens  

## 9. Voice & Tone
Friendly, concise, Iraqi Arabic  
Simple and useful  

## 10. Brand Positioning
Modern, trustworthy, clean, accurate, fast.

# Mawjood Logo Design Guide

## 1. Brand Concept
The Mawjood logo represents presence, accuracy, and local identity. It must feel modern, minimal, and instantly recognizable in Iraqi markets. The design should convey trust and clarity.

## 2. Core Concept Themes
- **Map Pin + Arabic Letter م**: A hybrid mark symbolizing location and identity.
- **Minimal Geometric Shape**: Clean, bold, and scalable at small sizes.
- **Single-Color Design**: Ensures clarity in all environments.

## 3. Logo Styles
### Primary Logo
- Icon: Abstract map pin formed using the Arabic letter **م**.
- Color: Teal (#00897B).
- Shape: Rounded, geometric, simple.

### Secondary Logo (Monochrome)
- All-white icon on teal background.
- All-black icon for documents or grayscale printing.

### Wordmark
- Arabic: **موجود**
- Font: Cairo / Noto Kufi Arabic (bold weight).
- Wordmark must never replace the icon but can accompany it horizontally.

## 4. Clear Space
Maintain padding equal to **25 percent of the icon's width** around all sides.  
No text, buttons, or UI elements may intrude.

## 5. Minimum Sizes
- Mobile app icon: 1024 x 1024 px (exported to platform sizes).
- Minimum display size: 24 x 24 px.
- Recommended use size: 32–64 px for headers, 48–80 px for splash screens.

## 6. Color Usage
### Primary
- **Teal**: #00897B (main identity)
### Secondary
- **White**: #FFFFFF  
- **Black**: #1A1A1A  

### Accent
- **Gold**: #FFB300 (sparingly, optional)

## 7. Background Rules
- Logo should not be placed on busy images.
- Avoid gradients behind logo.
- Use only:
  - White
  - Teal (#00897B)
  - Soft Sand (#F5F3E7)

## 8. Incorrect Usage
Never:
- Rotate the icon.
- Stretch or distort proportions.
- Apply gradients.
- Add shadows or bevels.
- Change the icon’s outline thickness.
- Place on low-contrast backgrounds.
- Combine with unapproved fonts.

## 9. Export Formats
The logo must be exported in:
- **SVG** (primary format)
- **PNG** (transparent background)
- **PDF** (print)

Export sizes should include:
- 24px, 32px, 48px, 64px, 128px, 256px, 512px, 1024px.

## 10. App Icon Guidelines
### Android
- Adaptive icon with:
  - Foreground: Logo mark (white or teal)
  - Background: Solid teal (#00897B)

### iOS
- Full-bleed teal background
- Centered white or teal logo mark

## 13. Ownership Notes
The Mawjood logo is part of the Mawjood brand identity system.  
It should remain consistent across all platforms, including:
- Mobile app
- Website
- Social media
- Marketing materials
- App store listings
